Story · Discovery · Technical Win
This summit exists because the PSC role is more powerful than how we've been using it. Your job isn't to deliver a great demo — it's to own the technical win. That means understanding your prospect's world deeply, telling a story that lets them see themselves in the solution, and getting their explicit agreement that it meets their needs before the deal moves forward.

How the Week Works

Monday: Some people can arrive on Monday evening. No organized sessions — just settle in.

Tuesday: Local arrivals and free time to work in the morning, lunch at noon (Office cafe), sessions 1:00 PM – 5:30 PM on discovery and presentation skills, then something in the evening from 7 PM.

Wednesday: Full day, Breakfast avaliable from 6.30 AM to 08:15 AM, then starting sessions at 9:00 AM with hands-on demo rebuild, live demos, practice, and prep for Thursday. Team dinner in the evengin somewhere nice.

Thursday: Final day. Breakfast avaliable from 6.30 AM to 08:15 AM, then starting sessions at 9:00 AM. Planning a finish around 4 PM leaving time to get to the airport.

Sessions are a mix of presentations, workshops, practice, and real performance.

Come ready to take risks, give honest feedback, and be fully present.
